## CALCULATE YOUR KEY NUMBERS:

**Conversion Rate:** (Leads ÷ Website Visitors) × 100 = _____%

**Meeting Rate:** (Meetings ÷ Leads) × 100 = _____%

**Win Rate:** (Wins ÷ Meetings) × 100 = _____%

**Time per Lead:** Total Hours Spent ÷ Total Leads = _____ hours

**Best Lead Source:** _____________ (generates highest quality at lowest time cost)

## LEAD QUALITY SCORE:

Rate each lead 1-5:
- **5**: Perfect fit, ready to move forward
- **4**: Good fit, needs some nurturing
- **3**: Possible fit, needs more qualification
- **2**: Poor fit, but might refer others
- **1**: Not a fit, disqualify

This helps you focus on highest-quality leads!
