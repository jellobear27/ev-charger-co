## TROUBLESHOOTING

**"I'm not getting any leads"**
- Are you reaching out to at least 10 people per day?
- Is your website mobile-friendly?
- Is your CTA clear on every page?
- Are you tracking where visitors drop off?

**"I don't have time"**
- Start with just 30 min/day
- Focus on highest-impact activities (outreach + content)
- Batch similar tasks together
- Use templates and systems

**"I don't know what to post"**
- Share customer success stories
- Answer common questions
- Share industry news with your take
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Tips and how-tos

**"My content isn't getting views"**
- Are you promoting it on social media?
- Are you using keywords people search for?
- Are you sharing in relevant groups?
- Are you engaging with others' content first?
